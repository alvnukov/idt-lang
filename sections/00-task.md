## 0. Protolanguage Task

The goal is to create a protolanguage in which the primitive notions are:

- event;
- distinguishability;
- inheritance of distinguishability;
- readout compatibility;
- facticity;
- stable invariants.

Quantum mechanics and relativity are not taken as starting languages.

They should arise as readout modes:

$$
\text{inherited distinguishability}
\Rightarrow
\text{QM readout}
$$

$$
\text{inherited distinguishability}
\Rightarrow
\text{relativistic / metric readout}
$$

This does not mean ignoring known physics.

On the contrary:

> every protolanguage object must pass a gate against known QM / relativity experiments and effects.

If no known-experiment gate is specified, the object has status:

`speculative_scaffold`

and not:

`accepted_theory_component`
